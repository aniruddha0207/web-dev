Evolvere

About Us

Official club of department of Biotechnology

Biotechnology is rapidly changing and advancing and is all set to be the key technological game changer in the 21st century. Our club evolvere provides a platform to the people with shared interest in biotechnology to nurture their ideas and it focuses on building a network that bridges the gap between the academia and biotechnology sectors. The club also organises various events,competitions & conducts workshops to foster ineterest in the field of biotechnology. In a nutshell, our aim is to educate the students about the advancements in biotechnology and encourage them to make some.

Events

FRESHERS

We, at EVOLVERE, organize the fresher's event, where the second years were formally welcomed to the Dept. of Biotechnology. The juniors had a fun time at getting to know their seniors, Evolvere, and what they should anticipate at this department. We at Evolvere wish our juniors the BEST OF LUCK!

TEACHERS' DAY

We at EVOLVERE are very excited to organize this event, Teachers Day. The juniors and seniors all come together to make this event a success. The students perform a few cultural activities followed by a cake cutting ceremony. We try to utilize this day that we dedicate to our teachers to the fullest.

BIOCOSMOS

We at EVOLVERE, had released a new E-magazine across various websites for biotechnology students. It included various topics such as potential job prospects, new lines of research and fun facts all in a single domain. We hope it was as informative and enjoyable to you as it was for us.

ALUMNI MEET

Here at EVOLVERE, we had cordially invited alumni members who shared their most heartfelt memories and imparted their wisdom to the current student body. They talked about how their four years in college were; some of their most transformative, challenging yet rewarding times. This reunion was certainly an event, which our alumni members looked upon fondly.

PRIONS

EVOLVERE recently started a 3 days fest that includes various competitions like quiz, debate and many more events. We wish to organize the same for you again.

FAREWELL

EVOLVERE organizes the farewell for the FINAL YEAR B.Tech batch every year. It is a time filled with uncertainty which no one was prepared for.We wished them good luck for their future endeavours.
